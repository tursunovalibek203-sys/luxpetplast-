## üéØ Copywriting Principles Applied

### 1. AIDA Formula
- **A**ttention: "Sizning Mahsulotingiz Uchun..."
- **I**nterest: "200+ kompaniya bizga ishondi"
- **D**esire: "FDA tasdiqlangan, muddatida yetkazish"
- **A**ction: "Bepul Konsultatsiya Oling"

### 2. PAS Formula
- **P**roblem: Implied (qadoqlash kerak)
- **A**gitate: "Sifat, muddatida, xavfsiz"
- **S**olve: "Bizning yechimimiz"

### 3. Feature ‚Üí Benefit
- Feature: "ISO 9001" ‚Üí Benefit: "Har bir idish bir xil"
- Feature: "200K quvvat" ‚Üí Benefit: "Katta buyurtma ham muddatida"
- Feature: "FDA" ‚Üí Benefit: "100% xavfsiz"
