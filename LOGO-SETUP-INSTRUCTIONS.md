### 2. TAVSIYA ETILADIGAN O'LCHAM

- **Minimal**: 200x200 piksel
- **Optimal**: 400x400 piksel yoki 500x500 piksel
- **Format**: PNG (shaffof fon bilan) yoki JPG

### 3. LOGONI SAYTGA QO'SHISH

Logoni saqlagandan keyin, quyidagi fayllarni yangilang:

#### A. index.html
```html

<img src="images/logo-final.svg" alt="LP Plast" class="logo">

<!-- Yangi -->
<img src="images/logo-lpplast.png" alt="LP Plast" class="logo">
```

#### B. Footer da
```html
<!-- Eski -->
<img src="images/logo-final.svg" alt="LP Plast" style="height: 40px; filter: brightness(0) invert(1);">

<!-- Yangi -->
<img src="images/logo-lpplast.png" alt="LP Plast" style="height: 40px;">
```
